A New Way to Learn Turkish Sign Language!

An interactive and effective way to learn sign language for those who want to learn sign language. It helps hearing impaired people improve their language skills with artificial intelligence-supported practices and real videos.

About

Mission and Vision

We want to make Turkish sign language accessible to everyone and to ensure the full participation of hearing impaired individuals in society. We aim to lead the formation of a society that learns and uses Turkish sign language, to support the rights of hearing impaired individuals and to progress to raise awareness in society.

Target Audience

Our application is designed for anyone who wants to learn Turkish sign language with artificial intelligence support. We target a wide audience including hearing impaired individuals, those who want to communicate with hearing impaired individuals, educators, students and linguists.

Objectives

  • Teach and popularize Turkish sign language.
  • Ensure the full participation of hearing impaired individuals in society.
  • Provide information to hearing impaired individuals about grammar and cultural backgrounds.
  • Increase communication with hearing impaired individuals in society and to make sign language more widespread.

Content

  • Basic sign teaching and practical materials.
  • Videos, interactive exercises and practical materials.
  • Artificial intelligence-supported interactive sign language application.

Key Features

AI-powered vocabulary practice.
Developing sign language comprehension skills with real videos.
Flexible and user-friendly interface.
Easy access and use.
